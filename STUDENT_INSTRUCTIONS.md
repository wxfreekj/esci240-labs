# ESCI 240 Student Instructions

## Before You Start

- Open the correct lab link from the course page.
- Use a modern browser such as Chrome or Edge for the best experience.
- Make sure your internet connection is stable before you begin.
- No software installation is required.

## How to Complete a Lab

1. Read the directions at the top of the lab carefully.
2. Answer each question directly in the browser.
3. Save your work as you go if the lab provides a save option.
4. Review your answers before exporting.
5. Click **Export Answers** when you are finished.

## How to Submit

- The export will create a `.txt` file.
- Upload that `.txt` file to Canvas.
- Submit only the file generated by the lab unless your instructor says otherwise.
- If you are unsure whether the file exported correctly, reopen it and check that your answers are present.

## Good Habits

- Work through the lab in order unless the directions tell you otherwise.
- Do your own work and follow your course’s academic integrity rules.
- Double-check names, units, and labels before submitting.
- Use complete sentences when a written response is requested.
- Keep an eye on point values or required formats in each section.

## Troubleshooting

- If a page does not load, refresh the browser once and try again.
- If you still have trouble, try a different browser.
- If your export does not download, allow pop-ups and downloads for the site.
- If the lab appears broken, take a screenshot and contact your instructor.

## Getting Help

- Read the instructions at the top of the lab first.
- Check the course announcements for updates or known issues.
- Contact your instructor or TA if something still does not work.

## Final Reminder

When you are finished, export your answers and upload the `.txt` file to Canvas before the deadline.
