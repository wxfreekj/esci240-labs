Introduction to Meteorology - Fall 2025

Welcome to ESCI 240 Labs

These interactive lab exercises will help you explore fundamental concepts in meteorology and atmospheric science. Each lab includes hands-on activities, data analysis, and visualization tools. Complete the exercises and export your answers to submit on Canvas.

Available Laboratory Exercises

Lab 1

Density

Explore the relationship between mass, volume, and density in atmospheric science.

Lab 2

Energy Balance

Understand Earth's energy budget and the balance between incoming and outgoing radiation.

Lab 3

Atmospheric Structure

Examine temperature, pressure, and density variations through the atmosphere.

Lab 4

Moisture & Stability

Analyze humidity, saturation, and atmospheric stability using thermodynamic diagrams.

Lab 5

Surface Observations

Learn to decode surface weather observations and plot station models.

Lab 6

Upper Air Analysis

Analyze upper-level atmospheric conditions using constant pressure charts.

Lab 7

Air Masses & Fronts

Identify air masses and frontal boundaries on surface weather maps.

Lab 8

Mid-Latitude Cyclones

Study the structure and evolution of extratropical cyclones.

 Lab 9

Severe Weather

Examine conditions favorable for thunderstorms, tornadoes, and severe weather.

Lab 10

Tropical Cyclones

Analyze the formation, structure, and forecasting of hurricanes and typhoons.
